Chimpanzee



Chimpanzees are one of the few animals that are known to use of tools. This includes the use of stones to open up appetising nut contents and leaves to soak up drinking water.

  • Kingdom: Animals
  • Class: Mammals
  • Order: Primates
  • Species: P. troglodytes

The Chimpanzee is a species of ape that is natively found in a variety of different habitats in western and central Africa. Closely related to other great apes including Orang-Utans and Gorillas, the Chimpanzee is also very closely related to Humans as we share 98% of the same DNA.
